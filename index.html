Travel Blog

I love to travel. To go places. To explore. I have been fortunate enough to visit a lot of great places and drive on magnificent roads. I have got a fair amount of passport stamps to go with it. I have experienced great cities such as Mumbai, New York, Boston, Seattle, Paris, Montreal, Toronto as well as famous natural parks such as Adirondacks, Yosemite, Crater Lake and Niagara Falls. I hope to keep expanding this list throughout my life. Take a glimpse into the traveling me in the posts below.

January 2020 | Paris, France

A Layover Special

A cheap flight ticket often comes with long layovers. I made the most of my 17 hours in Paris by exploring its majestic beauty at night. Eiffel Tower, Place-de-la-Concorde, The Louvre and Champ-de-Mars made the few hours look like eternity.

My roommates and I decided to explore the picturesque towns of Lake Placid and Lake George and hike to scintillating fall views of the Adirondacks. I also had the fortune of driving on some of the most scenic roads I have ever seen.
